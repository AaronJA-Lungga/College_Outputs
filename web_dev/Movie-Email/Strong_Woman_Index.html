Strong Woman Do Bong Soon (힘쎈여자 도봉순)

Do Bong Soon is a girl born with superhuman strength. In the world where the strong prey upon the weak, Bong Soon is by far the strongest predator in existence. This romantic comedy drama is about a young, reckless CEO, Ahn Min Hyuk, a just police officer, In Gook Doo, and a strong girl, Bong Soon.
